Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the capital city of Maharashtra and India’s financial, commercial, and entertainment capital, often called the "City of Dreams". Situated on the Konkan coast, it is a bustling, high-density metropolis, population nearly 20 million, known for Bollywood, theBombay Stock Exchange, and its colonial-era architecture
